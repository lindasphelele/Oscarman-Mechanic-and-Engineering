The workshop

Run by hand, not by guesswork.

Oscarman Mechanic and Engineering was founded in 2018 by Zama Oscar Mfusi in Utrecht, KwaZulu-Natal. What started as one mechanic with a toolbox has grown into a trusted local workshop that's repaired more than 50 vehicles from the same premises on Smith Street.

Every job gets the same approach: check it properly, explain what's wrong in plain terms, and fix it right the first time. No unnecessary parts, no rushed jobs.
